## Why We're Building Xenode

Cloud storage pricing hasn't made sense for Indian developers for a long time. Global providers optimize for Western markets—their pricing, infrastructure, and support all reflect that. If you're building from India, you're often paying more for worse latency.

We're building **Xenode** to change that. S3-compatible object storage, designed specifically for the Indian market.

## What Makes Xenode Different

### Optimized for India

- **Local infrastructure**: Your data stays in India
- **Lower latency**: Optimized routing for Indian networks
- **Rupee pricing**: No USD conversion, no surprises

### Simple Pricing

- **Storage** — ₹1.5 per GB/month
- **Egress** — ₹0.75 per GB
- **Requests** — ₹0.004 per 1,000 requests

No complex tiers. No "contact sales." You see exactly what you pay.

### Developer-First

- **S3-compatible API**: Works with aws-cli, boto3, rclone—everything you already use
- **Clean dashboard**: Built with Next.js and shadcn/ui
- **Good docs**: Because we actually use our own product

## Who This Is For

- Startups storing user uploads and documents
- Edtech platforms hosting course videos
- Agencies backing up client files
- Anyone tired of overpaying for basic storage
