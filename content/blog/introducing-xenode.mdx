## Why We're Building Xenode

Most cloud storage works like this: you upload a file, it lands on a server, and the company that runs that server can technically read it. They might say they don't. But they *can*.

We don't think that's good enough.

**Xenode** is built on a simple idea — your files should be encrypted before they ever leave your device. Not on the server. Not in transit. *In your browser*, before the upload even begins. By the time your file reaches our servers, it's already locked. We never hold the key.

## What Makes Xenode Different

### Your Key, Your Files

We use **AES-GCM encryption** handled entirely in your browser using the Web Crypto API. When you enable encryption, your files are locked with a key derived from your password. That key never touches our servers. If we were hacked tomorrow, attackers would find nothing but encrypted data they cannot read.

### Built for Everyone

Xenode is not just for developers. Anyone who cares about keeping their files private can use it:

- Store personal documents, photos, and sensitive files safely
- Share files with friends and colleagues using secure share links
- Set expiry dates and passwords on shared links
- Manage everything from a clean, simple dashboard

### Also Great for Developers

If you want to integrate storage into your app, Xenode has you covered:

- **API key access** with scopes and rate limits
- **Bucket and object management** via a clean REST API
- **Presigned URLs** for direct browser uploads
- **Usage metering** so you always know what you're consuming

### Simple, Honest Pricing

No complex tiers. No "contact sales". No USD conversion surprises. You see exactly what you pay, in rupees, upfront.

## Who This Is For
