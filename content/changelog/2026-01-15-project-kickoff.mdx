Welcome to the Xenode changelog.

We're building **end-to-end encrypted cloud storage** for people who want their files to stay truly private. Not private as in "we pinky promise not to look" — private as in *we literally cannot read your files* because your data is encrypted in your browser before it ever reaches us.

This changelog is where we'll share what we're working on: features, design decisions, security choices, and the occasional honest update about where things stand.

### Why a changelog?

We believe in building in public. Rather than going dark for months and dropping a big launch, we'd rather show you the progress week by week. It keeps us accountable and gives early supporters a real sense of where things are headed.

### What to expect

- Weekly to bi-weekly updates
- Deep-dives on how the encryption works
- Feature previews before they ship
- Honest updates on timelines and challenges

If you believe your files should be yours alone, [join the waitlist](/) and we'll make sure you're first in when we launch.
