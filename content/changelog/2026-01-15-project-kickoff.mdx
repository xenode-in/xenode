Welcome to the Xenode changelog.

We're building affordable, S3-compatible object storage optimized for Indian developers. This changelog is where we'll share what we're working on — features, infrastructure, design decisions, and the occasional behind-the-scenes look.

### Why a changelog?

We believe in building in public. Rather than going silent for months and dropping a big launch, we'd rather show you the progress week by week. It keeps us accountable and gives early supporters a real sense of where things are headed.

### What to expect

- Weekly to bi-weekly updates
- Technical deep-dives on infrastructure choices
- Feature previews before they ship
- Honest updates on timelines and challenges

If you're interested in what we're building, [join the waitlist](/) — we'll make sure early supporters get first access when we launch.
